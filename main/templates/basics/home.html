What is ApplyLog?

ApplyLog is a modern web app to manage your job applications with ease. Whether you apply through LinkedIn, Indeed, company portals, or referrals — keep everything tracked in one clean, simple space.

  • Organize all job applications in one place
  • Update progress & track your journey
  • Visual insights and performance metrics

Features You'll Love

Log Applications

Store role, company, platform, apply date — all in a few clicks. Keep your job hunt organized, always.

Track Progress

Easily move jobs from Applied to Interview, Rejected, or Offer. Your journey, clearly visible.

Insights & Analytics

Understand patterns, optimize your search, and measure success with beautiful charts and stats.
