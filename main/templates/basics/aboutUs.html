What is ApplyLog?

ApplyLog is your personal assistant in the job hunt. It lets you track every application — whether it's from LinkedIn, Indeed, Rozee or anywhere else — and monitor progress like a pro. No more lost emails or missed follow-ups.

  • 📌 Add applications in seconds
  • 🎯 See all statuses at a glance
  • 📅 Follow up without confusion
  • 🔒 100% privacy — your data, your rules

Built with a clear goal: to reduce stress, simplify tracking, and boost your chances of landing the dream job.
